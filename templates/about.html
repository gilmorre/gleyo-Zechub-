The Problem

Most community platforms optimize for spikes, not sustainability. Engagement fades after campaigns end, rewards are custodial and traceable, and analytics show vanity numbers that don't tell you why users leave.

Our Approach

Gleyo is built ZEC-native from the ground up. Rewards go directly to shielded Zcash wallets — no custodian, no other token. Analytics focus on real retention data: dropoff rates, growth opportunities, and risk alerts that actually help you act.

Built on Zcash

Powered by Zebra (Zcash full node) and Nozy Wallet API by LEONINE DAO for shielded Orchard transactions. Every reward, every interaction stays within the Zcash privacy umbrella.

Our Mission

To make community growth measurable, sustainable, and privacy-first — with ZEC as the native currency of contribution.
