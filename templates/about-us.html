About us

Built by people who felt the problem first

We weren't outsiders looking in. We were community members watching engagement die — and decided to do something about it.

Our mission

We believe every community deserves more than a follower count. Gleyo exists to give founders, creators, and teams the tools to build communities that actually last — where members stay, contribute, and grow together over time.

Where it started

Okiri and Olaide both came up as Zealy users — grinding quests, earning points, watching communities spike then go quiet. Okiri went deeper, managing one of the communities on Zealy himself, and saw firsthand how the tools fell short for anyone serious about long-term retention. That gap is exactly what Gleyo was built to close.
