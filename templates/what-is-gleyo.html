What is Gleyo?

The first ZEC-native quest and community growth platform built on Zcash.

Gleyo is a Zcash-native community growth and retention platform that helps founders, creators, and organizations build structured, measurable, and long-term engagement — with all rewards paid in ZEC, privately and non-custodially.

It combines ZEC-powered incentives, built-in community chat, and real retention analytics into one system designed for sustainable community growth on Zcash.

Who is Gleyo for?

  • Zcash and Web3 communities focused on long-term retention
  • DAOs and projects that want to reward contributors in ZEC
  • Creators building highly engaged privacy-first audiences
  • Teams running structured engagement and ZEC reward systems

What makes Gleyo different?

Unlike traditional platforms that use multi-token or custodial reward systems, Gleyo is ZEC-only and non-custodial — rewards go directly to users' shielded Zcash wallets with no middleman and no traceable on-chain footprint.

Instead of vanity metrics like Discord members and Twitter followers, Gleyo shows you real retention data — dropoff rates, retention percentages, and actionable growth signals that tell you exactly why users leave and what keeps them.

Built on Zcash

  • All rewards are denominated and paid in ZEC on Zcash mainnet
  • Users connect and authenticate with their Zcash wallet only
  • Withdrawals are processed as shielded Orchard transactions via Nozy Wallet API
  • Admins can reward active community members directly in ZEC from within the platform
  • Powered by Zebra (Zcash full node) and Nozy Wallet by LEONINE DAO

FAQs

What is Gleyo?
Gleyo is a ZEC-native quest and community growth platform built on Zcash — users connect their Zcash wallet, complete quests, and withdraw rewards in ZEC directly to their shielded address.
Who is it for?
Zcash communities, DAOs, Web3 projects, founders and creators who want to reward contributors privately in ZEC.
Why ZEC only?
ZEC is the only token with native shielded privacy at the protocol level. By being ZEC-only, Gleyo ensures every reward is private, non-custodial, and goes directly to the user's shielded wallet with no middleman.
What wallet do I need?
Any Zcash wallet that supports shielded Orchard addresses works — including Zold, Zkool, and Zingo. You simply connect your Zcash wallet address to receive ZEC rewards directly to your shielded address.
Is it free?
Early access is free for selected Zcash communities participating in the ZecHub ecosystem.
