Refund Policy

  • Payments on Gleyo can be initiated in USDT, USDC, or ZEC, but always settle on-chain in ZEC (Zcash).
  • If you pay with USDT or USDC, the ZEC amount shown on the invoice is a conversion locked in at the time the invoice is generated. That is the exact amount that must be sent — not the original USDT/USDC figure.
  • Due to the irreversible nature of blockchain transactions, completed and confirmed payments cannot be cancelled or reversed.
  • If you accidentally send an incorrect amount, contact support immediately. Assistance is reviewed case-by-case and is not guaranteed.
  • Payments sent to an incorrect wallet address cannot be recovered by Gleyo.
  • If a payment fails due to a platform error, eligible refunds will be issued in ZEC to the wallet address provided during review.
  • Blockchain network fees required to process a refund may be deducted from the refunded amount.
  • For refund inquiries, contact support through the Gleyo support system.
