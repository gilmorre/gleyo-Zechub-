Create your username

Choose a unique username that represents you on Gleyo. You can change it later from your settings.

Username guidelines:

• Usernames are case-sensitive and must be lowercase
• 3 to 20 characters long
• Only letters (a–z), numbers (0–9), and underscores (_) allowed
• No spaces or special characters

Example: gleyo_user
